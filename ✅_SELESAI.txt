💻 CARA MENGGUNAKAN
===================

Gunakan:

    imageUrl: coverProxy(imageUrl),  ← Hanya ini!
    fit: BoxFit.cover,
    placeholder: (c, _) => ShimmerBox(),
    errorWidget: (c, _, __) => ImageFallback(),
  )
